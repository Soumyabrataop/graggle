## Overview
This "One Shot" video is a comprehensive tutorial designed for Class 10 students to cover the entire chapter on Circles in a single session. It is typically used for exam preparation (like Board Exams) to review all concepts, theorems, and important questions quickly.

## Key Concepts Covered
1. **Introduction to Circles**: Basic definitions and properties.
2. **Tangent to a Circle**: Understanding tangents and points of contact.
3. **Number of Tangents from a Point on a Circle**: Theorems regarding the existence and properties of tangents.
4. **Theorems**:
    - Theorem 10.1: The tangent at any point of a circle is perpendicular to the radius through the point of contact.
    - Theorem 10.2: The lengths of tangents drawn from an external point to a circle are equal.
5. **NCERT Solutions**: Step-by-step solving of important questions from the NCERT textbook.
6. **Important Questions & PYQs**: Solving Previous Year Questions and likely exam questions to build problem-solving skills.

## Target Audience
- Class 10 students preparing for their Mathematics Board Exams.
- Anyone looking for a quick yet detailed review of the "Circles" chapter.
